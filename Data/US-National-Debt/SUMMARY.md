## Quick Context

The headline "national debt" is two debts: money borrowed on markets (debt held by the public) and IOUs between government accounts (intragovernmental, mostly Social Security). The economically meaningful measure is debt held by the public (~100% of GDP); the gross figure (~123% of GDP) is the one in headlines. Largest uncertainty: beneficial ownership of foreign-custodied holdings.
